The Future of smart-urban Farming

Smart urban farming reimagines how food is produced within city landscapes. Rather than relying on long-distance supply chains, crops can be cultivated in vertical farms, rooftop gardens, or repurposed industrial spaces using hydroponics or aeroponics. These methods drastically reduce land and water usage, eliminate the need for harmful pesticides, and shorten the distance from farm to table, thus lowering carbon emissions.
